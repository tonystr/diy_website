The Problem

Our planet is in danger of global warming, and the effects will be felt in all areas sooner or later. Greenhouse gas emissions are more than 50 percent higher than in 1990[1]. One of the largest causes of global warming appears to be human waste[2]. Read more

The solution

By empowering individuals with the tool of an AI that can identify electrical components and look up useful information on the fly, we believe we can make self-repair cheap and and easy, which would in turn lower the amount of wasted material[3]. Read more

UN SDG #13

Our solution is created to work toward the United Nations' Sustainable Developement Goal #13, however we realize our solution serves multiple of the UN SDGs, such as 4, 5, 8, 9, 10 and 12.
