[3] Electronic Waste SurveyORIGINAL RESEARCH

This is a survery we wrote in order to get quantitive data on how much people waste electronics, and to see if our solution could contribute to reducing those numbers. The survey was shared publically, however it's important to note that it was primarily shared in communities largely composed of technically interested people. This constitues a bias of interest and personality, which is a consequence of the communities and platforms we had access to. In the future, it would be beneficial to perform a similar study in a more random collection of people.

Source: https://no.surveymonkey.com/r/FZ7XT83
Authors: Bakstad A., Stigen V., and Strømsnæs T.
Date of downloading data: 25. November 2020
