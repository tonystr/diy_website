About this website

This website was developed as an exam-project for OsloMet university. We've done our best to make the website as accessible, reactive and comprehensible as possible, for as many different persons as we could. We've done extensive research, and attributed any work that our project builds on. All third party images are in public domain, from pexels.com.

Who are we?

We are a group of TKD students at OsloMet university, looking to apply ourselves in ways that ebenfit society as a whole.
