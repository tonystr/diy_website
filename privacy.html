Privacy & Terms

A brief summary of how we plan to handle
your privacy, and on what terms.

Does my data get sold/shared/publicised?

No. The AI is trained on our own data and voluntary testers, and only reacts to the video feed you provide it. The AI operates entirely locally, and only accesses the internet to look up guides and refer to our electronics database. You can opt-in to our test group, where you may choose to contribute data to improving the AI, but this is disabled by default. In other words, your private data is your private data, and it never leaves your device.

Why does the AI need access to camera and files?

The repair AI uses image-recognation to identify real-life objects. You can feed it single images, however it is a lot more accurate when operating on live camera feed, because it can see different angles, perceive depth, and correct for light issues. This requires a tremendous amount of memory, so instead of slowing your device to a halt, we ask to use a portion of your storage to store temporary data during use, which is later deleted.
