About this website

This website was developed as an exam-project for OsloMet university. We've done our best to make the website as accessible, reactive and comprehensible as possible, for as many different persons as we could. We've done extensive research, and attributed any work that our project builds on. All third party images are in public domain, from pexels.com. Inspect the source of this website on github.com.

Who are we?

We are a group of TKD students at OsloMet university, looking to apply ourselves in ways that benefit society as a whole.

Notes from development

Important events and decisions during development.

16. October 2020 -  Final Project exam was announced.
16. October 2020 -  Started planning, decided on idea, started reading and saving sources.
18. October 2020 -  Decided on theme, started developing frontpage.
20. October 2020 -  Developed tool reccomendations & store products.
24. October 2020 -  Developed store page.
28. October 2020 -  Found common ground on subpage layout & style through use of non-violent communication. Implemented prototype.
31. October 2020 -  Started writing Privacy & Terms.
7. November 2020 -  Decided to delete Store page, reccomended tools and products. Partly because the store relied on JavaScript, but mostly because we found that this was all secondary to our project's goal. We decided being concise and precise about our project was better than having complimentary pages and content.
12. November 2020 -  Created and shared survey related to our idea.
18. November 2020 -  Made website reactive and mobile-friendly.
21. November 2020 -  Found damnig statistics on electronic waste in norway.
21. November 2020 -  Did self-evaluation and group-evaluation.
23. November 2020 -  Finally decided on branding (project was called "DIY Stuff" until this).
24. November 2020 -  Decided to keep the language dropdown as a <select> element. We discussed about accessibility and style, but concluded that a built-in html element with javascript would work best most places, and that the fallback, a normal link, would substitute it wherever javscript isn't supported.
25. November 2020 -  Collected survey results, compiled them into a document and updated our website content.
26. November 2020 -  Translated all English content to Norwegian
27. November 2020 -  Made voice-over for English content.
27. November 2020 -  Made this webpage (About Us) and cleaned up the footer.
28. November 2020 -  Exchanged project with fellow exam group (Ulrik Singsaas [s351917] & co.) for feedback, inspired by The Snowball Assignment.
28. November 2020 -  Implemented tooltip when hovering over sources. Inspired by wikipedia. Uses Javascript, but has aria-hidden.
29. November 2020 -  Implemented feedback from project exchange.
29. November 2020 -  Inserted product protype graphic.
30. November 2020 -  Handed in project for evaluation.
